Usefull Forge Mods

Some Usefull forge mods that can be paired with Raven B+ to improve your gameplay and experience! This can range from game optimizations to alt namagers to just generally a better time playing Minecraft while using Raven B+.

Click on the title of the mod to download, or scroll to the bottom and download them all!

Mods

Deluxe Sprint

Better sprint mod. To edit keybind go to: Options > Controls > Scroll down and look for "Toggle Sprint"

Perspective Mod

Allows you to rotate your view around your player, while not moving your players head. Like a better F5. To edit keybind go to: Options > Controls > Scroll down and look for "Perspective"

Hit Particles

Shows sharpness particles and allows a variable intensity. To edit mod settings type: "/hitparticles" in chat.

Powns Co-ords

Shows your X,Y and Z and more, and displays it on your HUD. To edit mod settings type: "/coordshud" in chat.

Reach Display

Shows your hits reach on your HUD. To edit mod settings type: "/reachdisplaymod" in chat.

Powns Armour HUD

Displays your armour on your HUD. To edit mod settings type: "/armourhud" in chat.

Powns Potion HUD

Displays your current potion status's on your HUD. To edit mod settings type: "/potionhud" in chat.

AutoGG

Says "GG" after a Hypixel game. To edit mod settings type: "/autoGG" in chat.

BDSM Lite

All round game improvments.

Bedwars Stats

Use "/bw [Players Name]" to check a players Bedwars starts while in a lobby!

Optifine

Better looking game, FPS improvments, Zoom and shaders. (RECOMENDED MOD)

Patcher

Patches some bugs in the game.

Player API

A player API needed for some of these modules.

Sk1er Old Animations

Reverts block hitting animations to 1.7 and some other 1.8.9 animations back.

Vanilla Enhancments

Improves gameplay, better FPS.

Time Changer

Changes the time to whatever you want.
